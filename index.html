About Us

At Surf & Turf Coffee, we believe coffee is best enjoyed together. Our little shop was created with one goal in mind: to be a warm and welcoming place where neighbors, friends, and travelers can slow down and feel at home.

Our name comes from the balance we love — the turf, rooted in the heart of our community, and the surf, bringing a relaxed, easygoing spirit. Just like the waves meet the shore, we hope every visit here feels like the perfect blend of comfort and connection.

We serve freshly roasted coffee and homemade treats with care, but what really makes us proud is the community gathered around our tables. Whether you're starting your morning, taking a midday break, or catching up with an old friend, there's always a seat waiting for you.

Surf & Turf Coffee — brewed with love, shared with friends.
